    Juhu Beach

    Smacked right near the city center, Juhu Beach is synonymous to Mumbai. The vast expanse of water stretching across the Arabian Sea glistening under the crimson sun and crashing in a starry night is what makes up for one of the most surreal beaches in Maharashtra. Get your kids to run around the shore in their flip flops or maybe lie under the sun soaking in the warmth and getting that much-wanted while you sip in bottles of beer. You can even go on for an unforgettable horse ride along the shore. And if that wasn’t enough to make your visit an enthralling one, the vendors serving finger-licking-good street food do the job.

    Location: Vile Parle in Mumbai.

    Kashid Beach

    The group of beaches gathers another feather in their hat with this quintessential beach 125 km far from Mumbai. Looking for a chance to spend your weekend in paradise? Adorned with cliffs, rocks, and a golden shoreline, Kashid Beach is every beach baby’s, photographer’s, and nature lover’s escape. Care to go for a banana boat ride while you’re in there? Totally Recommended!

    Location: 125 km from Mumbai, 170 km from Pune

    Ganpati Pule

    Brace yourself to witness the combined timeless beauty of one of the Maharashtra beaches and pilgrimage village only a little far from Ratnagiri in Maharashtra. Being one of the two white sand beaches in Maharashtra, Ganpatipule Beach is a safe haven for photographers and pilgrims who visit Ganpatipule to offer prayers to Lord Ganpati. Oh, and by the way, if you happen to visit this sunbathing hub between the months of November and May, you have got to enjoy the thrill of varied water sports.

    Location: 24 km from Ratnagiri in Ganpatipule Village in Maharashtra.

    Velas Beach

    Up for an offbeat exploration of Maharashtra Beaches? How about witnessing the spectacular Olive Ridley Turtle Hatchlings coming out of the beach to bask in the fresh breeze along the shore. Get away on a weekend to this sojourn place and experience the power and beauty of nature and beaches to refresh and offer an enthralling experience. Book a homestay on the beach and make memories while you sit under a starry sky and marvel at the beauty of waves crashing and calming all at once.

    Location: Ratnagiri in Maharashtra.

    Harihareshwar Beach

    Step on the shores of one of the lesser known Maharashtra Beaches. Harihareshwar beach is a perfect blend of spirituality and holiday spirit. With a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva in its vicinity and 4 mountains embracing the beach, it just can’t get any better. Visit the Harihareshwara temple glorified with the background of Sahyadri hills and later bring down your packed lunch to spend time picnicking on the beach. Makes up for an eventful day, doesn’t it?

    Location: Harihareshwar in Maharashtra.

    Coastal-Karnatka

    Gokarna Beach

    Enjoy a pristine stretch of shoreline at one of the cleanest beaches in Karnataka. Gokarna Beach is slowly gaining popularity among tourists as well as locals for its panoramic vistas and spectacular coastline. The water is clean and perfect for a relaxing swim. Although Gokarna is not that hot because of its proximity to the sea, a dip in the clear blue sea is guaranteed to take away all your exhaustion. If you are are on a trip with the family, the beach offers the perfect spot for celebrating a picnic, as it is surrounded by emerald green forests. For the adrenaline junkies, you can try rock climbing too.

    Location: Gokarna Beach Road Dandebagh, Gokarna, Karnataka
    Distance from Bangalore: 485 km
    Activities: Swimming, sunbathing, picnic, rock climbing
    How to reach: The beach is less than a kilometre from the bus stand, so you can simply walk there.

    Tannirbavi Beach

    Spend a blissful hour or two at the serene and tranquil Tannirbavi Beach of Mangalore. Encircled by tall trees and offering some of the most beautiful sunsets in the region, the beach is famous among locals and tourists for its calm environs. People often go here in the evening to spend a few hours introspecting and connecting with nature. The sight of ships entering and exiting the harbour, boats riding the waves, and tiny fish that are visible through the clear water make this place a nature lover’s paradise. The beach also has public toilets, stalls and shacks selling snacks, and concrete benches to sit on.

    Location: Tannirbhavi Beach Kasba Bengre, Mangalore
    Distance from Bangalore: 357 km
    Activities: Boating, swimming, relaxing by the beach
    How to reach: The beach is 17 km from the beach and you can reach there by taxi.

    Murudeshwar Beach

    Murudeshwara is a holy city dedicated to Lord Shiva, situated in the southern state of Karnataka, on the coast of the Arabian Sea. This city mainly prides itself on having the second tallest sculpture of Lord Shiva in the whole world. Murudeshwara is easily accessible by bus, train and by air. The nearest airport is Mangalore airport, which is 133km away from the city. All the trains that connect Mangalore and Mumbai pass through the Murudeshwar Railway Station, which makes it conveniently accessible by frequent trains. Other nearby cities like Kochi, Bangalore and Mumbai are connected to Murudeshwara through very efficient bus service. The romantic sunsets on the Murudeshwar Beach are truly something worth witnessing when you are in the area.

    Location: Murudeshwar Beach, Murudeshwar

    Kodi Beach

    Nestled away in the quaint town of Kodi Bengere is one of the most spectacular beaches you will ever see in Karnataka. As it is somewhat far away from the city and does not attract many tourists, you will get to enjoy the flavours of the local fishermen life here. The secluded beach is also peaceful and quiet - the perfect place to spend an evening reading your favourite book. Enjoy the silence of nature as you walk along the shoreline and witness the fishermen casting their nets into the majestic sea. If you are seeking an adrenaline rush, you can go rock climbing. At the top of the cliffs, you will be rewarded with a breathtaking view of the Arabian.



    Location: Udupi, Karnataka
    Distance from Bangalore: 415 km
    Activities: Swimming, rock climbing, nature walk
    How to reach: The beach is 18 km from the bus stand and you can take a taxi to get there..

    Devbagh beach

    Enjoy the remarkable sights and enthralling beauty of Devbagh Beach in Karwar. The beach faces clear blue waters of the Arabian Sea on one side and the dense jungles of the Western Ghats on the other side. It is a private beach, cut off from civilisation, and that is why people come here to connect with themselves. If you are travelling with your beloved, you can take a romantic walk down the golden sandy shoreline. Or if you are an adventure sports enthusiasts, you can try the host of adventure sports activities available here, like snorkelling, banana boat rides, and kayaking. The island beach also has some dolphins, so you can swim with these cute and cuddly creatures too.

    Location: Karwar
    Distance from Bangalore: 357.8 km
    Activities: Parasailing, kayaking, snorkelling, banana boat rides, water scooter rides, speedboating, fishing, dolphin watching.
    How to reach: The beach is just 800 meters from the bus stand and you can simply walk to the destination. You can also take a boat from nearby beaches.
